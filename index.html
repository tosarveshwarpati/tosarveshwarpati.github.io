About me

Physics Scholar with good skills in Quantum Theory, Mathematical Methods and Experiment design at graduate level. Interested in both Theoretical and Experimental Physics.

I work in laser cooling and spectroscopy laboratory in Warsaw University for production and precision spectroscopy of ultracold CsAg molecule. I absolutely love my work.

What i'm doing

  • Density Matrix based Spectroscopic Calculations

    Optical Bloch Equations offers a precise way to calculate spectroscopic properties of multilevel quantum system.

  • Programming

    Junior Physicists do a Lot of Programming. (but senior physicists/supervisors dont!)

  • Electronics And Control

    A PhD student's life is filled with a myriad of electronics and control system design work.

  • Spectroscopy / Imaging

    Ultimately I am performing laser based spectroscopy and imaging experiments, life is over.

    Research Asst., Feb2021 - Oct2022

    During Feb2021-Oct2022; one and half year, I was engaged with optical laser induced breakdown spectroscopy spectroscopy and spectral data analysis as well as simulations of atomic spectral lines, electro-mechanical optical components and interfacing them with PC, electronics assignments from ongoing liquid spectroscopy laboratory establishments, writing computer programs etc. I have designed and prepared a spectroscopy chamber with variable axial electric field, to study effects of perturbative electric field on laser induced Plasma. Fun Stuff

Apart from these research activities, I have also volunteered to popularize/teach some physics courses to the engineering (but also science) students who want to come to higher studies in physics. The courses I have taught during the last two years are Lagrangian Physics, Second Quantization, and Fourier Transforms. I am also a teaching assistant to a currently running online course on quantum field theory, organized by Infosys Science Foundation.

My secondary interests also involve foundations and representations of QM, basis-dependent units of complex scalar fields elements, Hilbert spaces, Linear Algebra, old quantum theory, design/demonstration of home experiments, extraction of motivational/philosophical concepts from mathematics (Parseval-Plancherel identities, CSCO, Completeness, etc.), Linux bash, electronic circuits...
